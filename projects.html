AI Hate Speech Detector

For this project, I collaborated with two others to create two models of hate speech detection and then compare the results. Our data was collected from Kaggle. The first was a naive bayes model which achieved 88% accuracy for hate speech detection. The second model was a randomized forest model from scikit learn which achieved 82% accuracy for hate speech detection. Check it out here!
